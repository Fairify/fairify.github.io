Cookie Policy

At Fairify, we use cookies to enhance your user experience and to analyze website usage. This policy explains the types of cookies used, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

Necessary Cookies

We use necessary cookies to ensure that our website works correctly. For example, we use cookies that check if you are accessing the website on a mobile device to display the site correctly.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to collect information about how many visitors our site receives, including the average page views; This helps us understand how visitors use our website and improve its performance.

For more information on how we use analytics cookies, refer to our Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party cookies, such as "sp," to help us analyze website usage. These cookies expire after one year. We also use two third-party cookies called "S" and "COMPASS" as part of our embedded Google form. These cookies expire after an hour and are for creating new case submissions.

Functionality Cookies

​We use functionality cookies, such as "language," to store language preferences and serve content in the stored language. These cookies expire after 14 days.

Advertising and Marketing Cookies

We do not use advertising or marketing cookies on our website.

First-Party Cookies

We use several first-party cookies, including "_snow_ses.f42d" and "_snow_id.f42d," for analytical purposes. These cookies expire after 30 minutes and two years, respectively. We also use a first-party cookie called "is_mobile" to determine if you are on a mobile device and display the website correctly. This cookie expires immediately after the session.

Managing Your Preferences

When you visit our website, you will receive a pop-up banner at the bottom of the page asking you to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your preferences by clicking on the "Cookie Preferences" button in the banner. You can also manage your settings through your browser preferences.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cookie Policy, contact us at fairifymoderation@gmail.com.
