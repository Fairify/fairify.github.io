Donations

Thank you for considering a donation to Fairify!

Fairify is an organization dedicated to fairness and impartiality in online communities. At Fairify, we believe that moderators should approach every action with professionalism and honesty.

Your support, no matter the amount, will help us to continue providing a platform where users and moderators can submit cases for review to ensure fairness and balance. Your contribution will go towards affording a domain name, which is essential for our continued operation.

When you donate to Fairify, you're supporting our mission of offering a platform where users and moderators can ask for a review, giving everyone a fair chance to have their say. Our team is committed to providing an honest and thorough review of every case that comes our way.

Your generosity will help us to become the go-to spot for anyone needing a fair and impartial take on a moderation decision. Thank you for your consideration and for supporting us in bringing balance and fairness to the online community, one decision at a time.
