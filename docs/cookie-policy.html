Cookie Policy



At Fairify, we may use cookies to enhance your user experience. This policy explains the types of cookies used, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

Necessary Cookies


Cloudflare
We use Cloudflare cookies to identify individual clients behind a shared IP address and apply security settings per client. These cookies are necessary for the security and performance of our website.

Fastly CDN
Fastly CDN cookies speed up page load times and enhance your experience by distributing content delivery across multiple servers.


Functionality Cookies


Font Awesome
We use Font Awesome cookies to load icons and graphics that enhance our website's visual appeal and user interface.

GitHub Pages
We use GitHub Pages cookies to provide and improve the hosting services of our website, ensuring efficient and reliable access.


Analytics Cookies


We do not use analytical cookies on our website.


Third-Party Cookies


We do not use third-party cookies on our website.


Advertising and Marketing Cookies


We do not use advertising or marketing cookies on our website.


First-Party Cookies


We do not use first-party cookies on our website.


Managing Your Preferences


When you visit our website, you will receive a pop-up banner at the bottom of the page asking you to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your preferences by clicking on the "Learn more" button in the banner. You can also manage your settings through your browser preferences.

Updates to Our Cookie Policy


We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will notify those who have opted-in to receive policy updates via email.

Contact Us


If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cookie Policy, contact us at fairifymoderation@gmail.com.
