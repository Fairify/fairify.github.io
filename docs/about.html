Our Mission

Dedicated to Communities

At Fairify, we understand how crucial fairness and impartiality are in moderating online communities. That's why we're here, to give a second opinion and ensure fair moderation decisions.

Our mission is to offer a place where people and moderators alike can ask for a review, giving everyone a fair chance to have their say. We think every decision should be neutral with the best level of professionalism and honesty, and that's what we aim to deliver.

We're a group of volunteers committed to offering a fair and thorough review of every case that comes our way. Our goal is to be the go-to spot for anyone needing a fair and impartial take on a moderation decision.

So if you're looking for a second opinion on a moderation call, look no further than Fairify. We're here to bring balance and fairness to the online community, one decision at a time.

The Issue

Power Abuse

Many online communities face challenges that can cause distrust among their members, including bias, lack of transparency, inconsistent moderation actions, and sometimes even rogue moderators.

There have been several instances where moderators have applied their biases and ideologies in the way they moderate, often punishing members who disagree or criticize their actions or responses.

Being a moderator naturally introduces a feeling of power. The issue is that some moderators abuse this newfound power for personal benefit.

For example, a moderator may push their misguided ideologies upon community members and permanently ban those who disagree or oppose their view, even if they didn't violate the rules.

Our Solution

A Fair and Unbiased Second Opinion

Allowing a reliable service like Fairify to handle your community appeals can reassure your members that actions taken against them were fair and justified while providing more insight and transparency into the decision, which builds trust within your community.

Once a member submits an appeal, Fairify will initiate a comprehensive and transparent investigation to ensure that they followed the rules within the community and that any moderation action taken was reasonable.

When we conclude our investigation, a vote among our experienced board members will take place. We will review our findings and determine whether to reverse the moderation action or provide additional insight to the user who submitted the appeal to reassure them of the moderator's decision.

By introducing an experienced team of reviewers, we can successfully address the challenges many online communities face, providing members with more security by knowing that our team handles their appeals with the utmost care and objectivity.

Our Promise

Ensuring Fairness and Impartiality

We understand that many of the challenges we noted could raise concerns for a service like Fairify. How can you be sure that Fairify will remain unbiased and reliable?

Our board members are carefully selected to ensure they have experience in making justifiable moderation actions. After reviewing a case submitted to Fairify, we analyze our board members' feedback to ensure that their responses and critical thinking processes align with our core mission.

In the rare instance that a board member falls short of upholding our vision for Fairify, or our mission, we act swiftly. A review is conducted, and the board member is promptly removed through an internal vote, demonstrating our commitment to our mission.

We strive to promote fairness, and part of this proactive thinking means that we remain transparent and focused on our mission to promote justice in online communities across social media.
