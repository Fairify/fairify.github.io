Privacy Policy

At Fairify, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how we use, share, and protect that information.

Information We Collect

When you submit a case to us, we collect your email address, username, the platform on which the moderation situation occurred, and the server/subreddit where the moderation situation occurred. We use this information to contact you regarding updates on your case and to reference your username when coordinating an investigation with the moderators.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to anonymously track the average number of page views, overall visits, and unique visits. This information is used solely for statistical purposes and does not allow us to retrieve or store any personal information about the visitor.

Data Retention

We retain case information for three months before it is erased internally; This includes your email address, username, platform, server/subreddit, and case ID. After this period, your personal information is deleted permanently from our records.

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with anyone outside of our organization except in the following circumstances:
• If required to do so by law
• To our legal counsel and other advisors for the purpose of providing services to us
• To the moderators to coordinate an investigation of your case, we only share your username and not your personal information.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use a strong password and two-factor authentication for our main Fairify account, and we limit access to your personal information to only those employees who require it for their job.

Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify those who have opted-in to receive privacy policy updates via email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at fairifymoderation@gmail.com.
